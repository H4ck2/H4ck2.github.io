Disclosure policy

Vendors deserve the opportunity to fix issues before the public knows about them. That principle shapes every decision here - from first contact to final publication.

  • Contact the vendor privately before disclosing anything publicly.
  • Allow reasonable time to patch - typically 90 days, negotiable in good faith.
  • Withhold exploit details until a fix is available or the window closes.
  • Publish only what is necessary to inform defenders, not enable attackers.

Contact

For coordinated disclosure, reach out directly. Please include the affected product, a brief description of the issue, and your preferred timeline. Sensitive technical details should wait until a secure channel is established.
